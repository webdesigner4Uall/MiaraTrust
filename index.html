About

Vision Statement of Miara Trust : Miara Trust envisions a kind and inclusive society where compassion connects communities. We aim to serve those in need—be it stray animals, orphaned children, or the elderly— through regular acts of care, support, and service. By promoting empathy and responsibility, we strive to create a world where no soul is left behind, and every act of kindness makes a lasting difference.
